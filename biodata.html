Gordon Kyle Diez Cormier

Gordon Kyle Diez Cormier (born October 8, 2009[2]) is a Canadian teen actor. He played Joe in The Stand (2020) and portrayed Aang in the Netflix live-action Avatar: The Last Airbender series.

Life and Career

Cormier was born on October 8, 2009[2] and is of Filipino descent.[3] He grew up in Vancouver, British Columbia, and became interested in acting.[4]

He has starred in a Canadian cartoon series.[5] His debut role was in Get Shorty (2017), where he played Guatemalan Urchin. He also appeared in Lost in Space (2018).

His role in The Stand (2020) as Joe, a young boy who becomes the ward of Nadine Cross, brought him to mainstream attention.[6][7]

In August 2021, he was cast as the titular character Aang in Avatar: The Last Airbender, a Netflix live-action remake of the animated series of the same name.[8]
